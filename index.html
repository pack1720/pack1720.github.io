Information

  • Where does the pack meet?
    Milburn Park Pavillion
  • When are Pack Meetings?
    3rd Thursday of the Month from 6:30pm-8:00pm
  • Where do we get our Scouts from?
    Any child may join regardless of school. That said, most Scouts hail from Deer Creek Elementary. A close second is Westside Elementary. We also have Scouts who attend Naumann, Sterling Classical School and other nearby schools. Additionally, we have a share of Homeschooled children from the area as well.
  • Who can join?
    Any child, boy or girl, K-5 regardless of school
  • How can my child join?
    WRITE ME

More information

  • Which Boy Scout Troops do the Cubs Cross Over to?
    Cub Scouts may cross-over to any troop they choose to. There's no set troop. That said, the Troops that our cubs tend to mostly cross over to are: Troop 2008, Troop 271
  • How can I volunteer to help out?
    Any parent is welcome (and encouraged!) to volunteer to help the Pack. We need volunteer parents in all positions. Please come to a meeting and speak with a Pack Leader to hear about open positions.
  • I am an Eagle Scout parent
    We love Eagle Scout parents. Why? Because they volunteer to become our Den Leaders, Committee Members and Cubmasters. Please come to a meeting and speak with a Pack Leader to hear about open positions.
